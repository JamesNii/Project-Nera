The ROG Strix SCAR 17 is a powerful gaming laptop with AMD Ryzen 9 5900HX, GeForce RTX 3080, and a choice between 360Hz esports or WQHD 165Hz display.The laptop features a responsive optical mechanical keyboard for precise gaming inputs, providing a competitive edge for dominating any gaming arena.

DDR5 RAM

Leveraging the brand new DDR5 standard, enjoy a huge generational performance jump to 4800Mhz, with write times 50% faster than DDR4 equipped machines. This ultra-fast memory makes all aspects of the laptop feel snappy, from intense gaming to light web browsing.

CPU - AMD Ryzen™ 9 7945HX3D

3D Supremacy​

3D V-Cache technology is available for the very first time in a laptop. The Ryzen 9™ 7945HX3D offers double the L3 cache of its non-X3D equipped predecessor, leading to incredible performance gains in gaming on the same process node.

NVIDIA GeForce RTX™ 4090

With NVIDIA® Advanced Optimus, the laptop can automatically route frames from the discrete GPU directly to the display using a MUX Switch, bypassing the integrated graphics. This pathing improves performance by 5-10% compared to machines without a MUX Switch, making sure you get the absolute best gaming experience — all without needing touch a single button.
